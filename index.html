How to Play

  1. Click on any game in the grid to play it (opens in a new tab)
  2. After playing, enter your score (based on the par metric, e.g how many guesses it took, mistakes you made or hint you used). Squares score as follows:
    • Unattempted: Not attempted (0 points)
    • Flawless: First guess, full points, no mistakes (3 points)
    • Below Par: Better than par score (2 points)
    • Above Par: Worse than par but completed (1 point)
    • Failed: Failed to complete (-1 point)
  3. Complete rows, columns, or diagonals for bonus points!
  4. A new grid is generated daily
