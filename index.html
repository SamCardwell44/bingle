How to Play

  1. Click on any game in the grid to play it (opens in a new tab). Each game is assigned a difficulty, which determines the par score for that game.
  2. After playing, enter your score (based on the par metric, e.g how many guesses it took, mistakes you made or hints you used). Squares score as follows:
    • Unattempted: Not attempted (0 points)
    • Flawless: First guess, full points, no mistakes (3 points)
    • Below Par: Better than par score (2 points)
    • Above Par: Worse than par but completed (1 point)
    • Failed: Failed to complete (-1 point)
  3. Completed rows, columns or diaganols score as many points as squares in that row, column or diaganol. E.g in a 3x3 it is worth 3 bonus points.
  4. You have a limited number of game rerolls if there is a game you do not want to complete. Click on the reroll buttton and then the square you wish to reroll.
  5. A new grid is generated daily, or you can edit the seed below this to create a custom grid.
